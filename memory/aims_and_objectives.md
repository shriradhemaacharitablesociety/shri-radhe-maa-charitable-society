# Aims and Objectives

As per the Memorandum of Association (MOA), registered 21 August 2017.

## Education & Institutions
a. Establish, run and manage Schools, Colleges, Technical Institutions, Professional Institutions, Coaching Centres, Hospitals, Medical Colleges
b. Provide grants, scholarships, fellowships and financial assistance to needy/deserving students for education, vocational training, skill development
c. Financial assistance to educational institutions for scholarships, prizes, medals, awards for excellence in studies, sports, scientific research; distribution of books and notebooks
k. Provide for or contribute to education and scientific research and development
s. Encourage students in technical education, cultural and extra-curricular activities, value based education
t. Open public libraries with books on every aspect, especially technical subjects
w. Run educational institutions in various States/UTs under the respective educational acts
x. Establish and run Technical, Engineering and Medical Institutions
dd. Construct school buildings for children of economically weaker sections
ll. Non-formal education and literacy, especially for children and women

## Feeding & Basic Needs
d. Financial assistance for performing annadhanam (feeding) the poor directly and through institutions
g. Expenses and donations for feeding the poor and marriage of poor girls
h. Donations towards procurement of cycles, blankets, sewing machines, clothes to the needy and vagrant

## Healthcare
e. Organise camps for free medical check-ups, blood donation drive, book donation drive for backward and poor strata
bb. Setup, organise and operate Medical camps in rural areas
hh. Measures towards leprosy eradication and awareness
oo. Rural Health Programs
pp. Family Welfare and immunization

## Social Welfare
f. Financial assistance and aide to Gaushalas (cow shelters)
i. Establish, conduct, maintain old age homes, homes for physically challenged, and grant financial assistance to similar institutions
j. Grant donation to any Temple, Mosque, Church, Gurudwara and other places of worship / religious institutions
l. Financial assistance and aide to the poor and advancement of any object of general public utility
mm. Creation of employment opportunities for urban and rural population living below poverty line
nn. Supportive services for women — Creches/Balwadis for children of working women
ee. Construct dwelling units for economically weaker sections

## Environment & Rural Development
cc. Develop land and recover waste land with special concern on ecological improvement
ff. Soil and water conservation including harvesting of run-off water
gg. Rural and non-farm activities
qq. Tree plantation
rr. Social Forestry
ss. Development of Irrigation Resources
tt. Rural Sanitation — construction of low cost latrines
jj. Establishment and running of non-conventional and renewable sources of energy systems

## Culture, Faith & Community
m. Introduce and operate career guidance programmes
n. Establish contact with one another and develop good habits for leading healthy, happy and holy life
o. Teach the young generation the spirit of universal love, brotherhood, patriotism and service through lectures, workshops, seminars, conferences, exchange programmes
p. Promote brotherhood and faith in God through musical congregations, religious ceremonies, symposiums, discourses, seminars and conferences — keeping in view diverse Indian Cultures and Religions
q. Publish and distribute magazines, newsletters and brochures to develop literary taste among children and youth
r. Arrange exhibitions of arts and crafts, fine art, painting, and organise photographs and sports meet for all round growth and development of children/students
ii. Steps towards promotion of sports

## Organizational
v. Receive donations and aide from all over the world for advancement of objects
y. Assist, subscribe, cooperate, affiliate or amalgamate with any similar public body in India and abroad
z. Create or assist in creating branches for any of the purposes aforesaid
kk. Any other program for uplift of the rural poor or urban slum dwellers as the National Committee may consider fit
uu. The society may seek from the concerned authority/government department, land or ancillary objects at concessional rates
