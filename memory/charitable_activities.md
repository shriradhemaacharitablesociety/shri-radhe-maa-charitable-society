# Charitable Activities

Ongoing and recurring seva programs run under the guidance of Shri Radhe Guru Maa.

## Donation Drives
- **Blood donation** camps
- **Clothing distribution** to the underprivileged
- **Food distribution** (langar/bhandara)
- **Books and educational material** for students
- **Medicine distribution** for the needy

## Institutional Support
- **Sadhus and saints** — support for spiritual practitioners
- **Old age homes** — regular visits and donations
- **Religious scholars** — financial and material support
- **Cow shelters (Gaushalas)** — feeding and maintenance
- **Orphanages** — education, food, clothing
- **Schools** — infrastructure and supplies

## Environmental Initiatives
- Tree planting drives
- Promoting eco-friendly living
- Environmental awareness campaigns
