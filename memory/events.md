# Key Events & Celebrations

## Recurring Annual Events

### Janam Utsav (Birthday Celebration)
- **Date:** Around April 4 each year
- **Format:** Grand celebration with bhajan performances, celebrity guests, community gathering
- **Recent:** March 2026 — Bhajan Jamming & Janam Utsav at Borivali East, Mumbai
  - Featured artists: Manoj Tiwari, Kanhaiya Mittal
  - Major production with stage, lighting, and large devotee attendance

### Satsang / Bhajan Events
- Regular spiritual gatherings
- Bhajan sessions — devotional music with live performers
- Darshan events for devotees

### Charitable Seva Events
- Blood donation camps
- Food distribution drives (Bhandara/Langar)
- Clothing and book distribution events

## Key Locations for Events
- **Mumbai:** Radhe Maa Palace, Borivali West / Borivali East venues
- **Delhi:** Society HQ — Plot No. 5, Pocket-11, Sector-5, Rohini
- **Punjab:** Mukeria and surrounding areas

## Notes
- Events blend devotion with entertainment (Bollywood-style production values)
- Celebrity involvement is a hallmark — draws media attention and wider audience
- Event content performs well on Instagram — should be a key pillar of social media strategy
