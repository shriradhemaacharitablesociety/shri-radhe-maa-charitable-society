## Key Notes
- Office bearers elected from among **life members** only
- Term: **3 years**, eligible for re-election
- Quorum: 2/3rd of Managing Committee members
- President has **casting vote** in case of equality
- General Secretary can incur expenditure up to Rs. 5,00,000/- without MC approval
- President can incur expenditure up to Rs. 1,00,000/- for society welfare
