# Rules and Regulations

Summary of the Rules & Regulations of Shri Radhe Maa Charitable Society.

## 1. Membership

### Life Members
- All founding signatories (Clause 5 of MOA) are **life members**
- Any person contributing **Rs. 1,11,000/-** (One Lac Eleven Thousand) can become a life member, subject to Managing Committee approval
- Life members contribute Rs. 1,11,000/- each

### Ordinary Members
- Contribution of **Rs. 21,000/-** (Rupees Twenty One Thousand)
- Annual continuation fee: **Rs. 2,100/-**
- Enrolled by the Managing Committee

### Membership Requirements
- Must have attained age of majority
- Sound mind
- Not convicted of moral turpitude
- Interest in development of the society
- No discrimination of religion, caste, creed or colour
- Subject to Managing Committee approval

### Termination of Membership
- Death
- Failure to pay subscription within 4 weeks of due date (1st January)
- Working against society's aims and objects
- Resignation
- Failure to attend 3 successive General Body meetings (without intimation)

## 2. Managing Committee
- 7–10 members including office bearers
- Elected from life members only
- Term: 3 years
- Election by secret ballot
- Quorum: 2/3rd members
- Can co-opt members (no voting rights, don't count towards max)

## 3. Meetings

### Annual General Body Meeting
- Held in the **month of June** every year
- 10 days notice required
- Quorum: 2/3rd of General Body members
- If no quorum, reconvene after 15 minutes — members present deemed quorum

### Interim/Emergent Meetings
- On written request of 3/4th members
- At least 1 day prior notice (MC meetings)
- 3 days prior notice (General Body)

## 4. Advisory Board
- International character
- Representations from all important religion and cultural organisations
- Advises Managing Committee

## 5. Office Bearer Sub-Committee
- Considers all agenda before MC/Society meetings
- Consists of: President, General Secretary, Treasurer

## 6. Bank Account Operation
- Jointly by any **two** of: President, General Secretary, Treasurer
- Treasurer's signature **mandatory** on all cheques
- Bank accounts opened with Managing Committee consent

## 7. Financial Controls
- Accounts audited every year by MC-appointed auditor
- Financial year: 1st April to 31st March
- General Secretary places income/expenditure statement before General Body

## 8. Legal Framework
- Governed by Societies Registration Act, 1860 (as extended to Delhi)
- Amendments per S. 12 and 12-A of the Act
- Dissolution per S. 13 and 14 of the Act
- General Secretary may act/initiate legal proceedings per S. 6
