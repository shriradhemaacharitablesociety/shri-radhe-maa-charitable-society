# Shri Radhe Guru Maa

The spiritual leader and founder figure of the Shri Radhe Maa Charitable Society.

## Biography
- **Full Name:** Sukhvinder Kaur
- **Known As:** Radhe Maa / Mamtamai Shri Radhe Maa
- **Born:** April 4, 1965, Dorangala village, Gurdaspur district, Punjab
- **Years of Service:** 30+ years of humanitarian work

## Areas of Influence
Mukeria (Punjab), Mumbai (Maharashtra), Delhi, Chandigarh, Hissar (Haryana), and abroad

## Spiritual Philosophy
- God exists in all forms — Bhagwan, Allah, Jesus, Waheguru
- "Prem aur bhakti se Bhagwan prasann hote hai" (God is pleased by love and devotion)
- "Karm hi bandagi hai" (Work itself is worship)
- Serving all beings is serving God
- Devotees regard her as a mother and an avatar of Durga

## Headquarters
Radhe Maa Palace, Singh House, Borivali West, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400092

## Public Appearances
- Television: Guest on Bigg Boss 14
- Events: Annual Janam Utsav celebrations with celebrity bhajan performances
- Notable associations: Bollywood personalities, politicians, devotional artists
